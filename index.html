Seventeen is a South Korean boy band formed by Pledis Entertainment, which is now owned by HYBE Corporation.

The band consists of 13 members: S.Coups, Jeonghan, Joshua, Jun, Hoshi, Wonwoo, Woozi, DK, Mingyu, The8, Seungkwan, Vernon, and Dino.

The name Seventeen signifies the sum of the members (13), units (3), and the group as a whole (1).

They debuted on May 26, 2015 and have since released 4 studio albums, 12 mini albums, 2 special albums, 3 single albums, and 5 digital singles.

This includes their recent mini album called “Seventeenth Heaven” which consists of 8 songs.
